Overview

Sandy Miller is a photographer based in Essex who conducts various forms of the art, examples of such being the still life, fine art, and macro disciplines. Perhaps most distinguishing about Miller's work is the "bark art" album, which consists of close-up edits of a range of different types of tree branches. There are additionally the numerous works on sailing, such as of bawleys and barges, presented both in sport and fine art mannerisms.
