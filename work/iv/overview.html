Overview

Christian Hopkins is a portrait photographer, based in Philadelphia, who conducts under the performance and surrealism genres. He is most famous for the latter, which serve not only as expressions of his depression, but an overall mechanism of coping with it. Hopkins says; "“I've been suffering from Major Depression for the past 4 years and it has manifested itself throughout that period in many ways, photography included.”
