Overview

Cenci Goepel and Jens Warnecke comprise Lightmark, based in the state of Mecklenburg-Vorpommern in Germany. The duo entirely conduct and are most renowned for their unique fusion of long exposure and landscape photography, wherein moving sources of light are captured within natural environments at very long exposures at night to create surreal images. Their work is diverse, with a range of paintings and locations, the latter of which including Australia, Argentina, Norway, and America.
