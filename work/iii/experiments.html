Experiments

IMG_2233

An attempt at iridal manipulation, entailing alteration of the brightness and colour of the subject's irises. Also present is an effort to print text in repeated fashion onto a surface, in this case the sheet, entirely within editing.

IMG_0382

A similar attempt to the former experiment, though instead an animation of the irises slowly oscilating to and from a set colour and saturation, on an edited image. An error in the studio lighting setup resulted in the presence of whites being too strong and 'bleaching', compromising part of the subject's face. I attempted to rectify this through extensive selective tonal gradienting and healing.
