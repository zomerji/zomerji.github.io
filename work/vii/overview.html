Overview

Russ Ellis is a freelance cycling photographer from the UK, who takes photographs for the Ineos Grenadiers (a leading professional cycling team) and Specialized (one of the most popular bike brands). His photographs intend to capture the intense emotions within the sport in an almost artistic form, as well as the action present in the races. Ellis’ photographs are very ‘surface level’, in that they are interpretationally simple and therefore easier for the viewer to interact with, both on an aesthetic, and emotional level.
