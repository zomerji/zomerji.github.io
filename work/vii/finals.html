The seventh shoot has been a revisiting of a part of last years' work, which explored sport photography. This effort has been to not only apply my present skillset and ability to a past project, but also to expand on it beyond its original constraints. A few of the above finals have been 'remasters' of the original edits (though more in the sense of having been redone than retouched), but the others are entirely new takes on the original photographs.
