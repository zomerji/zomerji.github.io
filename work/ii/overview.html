Overview

Candida Höfer is a German architecture photographer from Cologne, who captures the most aesthetic and iconic exemplars of architecture across the world. Her work is easily recognisable as being very high-depth-of-field and wide-angle, including a balanced mixture of tones and colours, and typically being perspectively symmetrical. Therefore, her photography can be described as technically perfect and conceptual. Additionally, the pictures are more fundamentally realistic, as their elemental and structural composition is reminiscent of a human viewpoint, albeit perhaps enhanced.
