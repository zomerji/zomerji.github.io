These are the finals for the second shoot. Akin to the first, at no point did I have any intention of replicating or even mimicing Calio's work. All ten photographs are derived from my interpretation of his photographs, and my own reapplication.

Evident on many of these photos are an arbitrary blur which was caused by an amassment of raindrops on the lens during the period of rain in the shoot, which I decided to leave on for the most part to experiment with as a natural form of bokeh.

And as per the first shoot, I will not cover techniques.
